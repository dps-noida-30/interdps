BRANDS CONTRIBUTION IN SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT

INDIAN BRANDS

No Nasties

No Nasties primarily uses organic cotton to make their clothing. Organic cotton is grown without the use of synthetic pesticides and fertilizers, promoting soil health and reducing environmental impact.

The Terra Tribe

Sustainable clothing brands like Terra Tribe often focus on ethical sourcing of materials and ethical production practices. This may involve using organic or eco-friendly materials and ensuring fair wages and safe working conditions for their workers.

Decathlon

Decathalon strives to reduce their carbon footprint by implementing energy-efficient processes, utilizing renewable energy sources, and optimizing their supply chain. This includes employing recycled or environmentally friendly components in their products to reduce their environmental impact.

MORE BRANDS

Patagonia

Patagonia is a pioneer in sustainable fashion. The brand emphasizes long-lasting, high-quality items and encourages repair and recycling through initiatives like as the "worn wear" campaign.

Reformation

It is well-known for its transparent and sustainable methods. They use sustainable materials and prioritize minimizing waste and water usage in their manufacturing operations.

Thought

It is committed to using environmentally friendly materials such as organic cotton, tencel, and recycled fibers. In addition, for every t-shirt sold, a tree is planted.

Veja

Veja makes sneakers out of eco-friendly materials including organic cotton and wild rubber from the Amazon jungle. The brand prioritizes long-term transparency and ethical labor standards.
